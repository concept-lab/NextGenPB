Initializes and updates markers for quadrants in a forest mesh. 

This function sets up markers for quadrants within a forest mesh to distinguish whether they are inside, outside, or on the boundary of a molecule. It also computes dielectric properties and reaction rates for the nodes based on their location relative to the molecule and optional Stern layer.


\begin{DoxyParams}{Parameters}
{\em ray\+\_\+cache} & A reference to a ray tracing cache structure that holds information on required rays and their intersections.\\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
This function performs the following\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Initializes the dielectric constants ({\ttfamily epsilon}) for the inside and outside regions.
\item Computes reaction rates based on ionic strength and other physical parameters.
\item Loops over quadrants in the mesh, evaluating the position of nodes relative to the molecule and Stern layer (if present).
\item Updates markers for quadrants and nodes based on their location\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\ttfamily 0.\+0}\+: Inside the molecule.
\item {\ttfamily 0.\+5}\+: On the boundary of the molecule.
\item {\ttfamily 1.\+0}\+: Outside the molecule.
\end{DoxyItemize}
\item Updates reaction and dielectric properties for nodes inside the molecule or Stern layer.
\item Handles MPI-\/based parallelism, including barrier synchronization and data exchange.
\item Ensures rays are calculated and cached for points near molecular boundaries.
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{structpoisson__boltzmann_autotoc_md4}{}\doxysubsubsubsection{\texorpdfstring{Stern Layer Handling}{Stern Layer Handling}}\label{structpoisson__boltzmann_autotoc_md4}
